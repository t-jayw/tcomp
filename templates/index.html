Please Take Care Of My Plant

This is the internet's plant, please take care of it.

How it works

Every day YOU can vote whether or not to water the plant on that day.

Voting takes place on the dedicated subreddit, r/takecareofmyplant

The livestream is a great way to check in on the plant and see how it is doing.

Scroll down to see some data and learn more about the plant and this project!
